A true Life of a Queen

                  At about noon african time, there was a great rain fall in the city of Abuja Nigeria on an election day where a new government was sworn in. Everyone was scared of the sudden changes and tribulations which left many Nigerian citizens in great grif,fear and regret. Most citizens testified it was a sign of a bad omen because of how strange and sudden it all occured in just less than 24hrs. Prices of food stuff became unaffordable for the masses life became worst and the future became so dark and dozzy. These unbearable conditions led to the lost of lifes and properties, kidnapping and fruad became the only source of survival because there was no hope for the youths and the masses but its said that the youth are the leaders of tomorrow which turned the mind of the masses never to believe in that again. At exactly 10pm in the night a beautiful woman was rushed to the pharmacist by her only son for some medical care,tho she was ill and diagnosed of maleria fever. After some couple of hours she passed away because the health personal refused treating her for some financial reasons then life became meaningless without the existance of a mother. The only son of the beautiful woman was abandoned by friends and family which made the orphan more stronger, dedicated and never to giveup on his dreams.
                  Few months later, he met a beautiful woman on the green app named Priscila who gave him every reason to smile again and every reason to be succesful. she inspired him, gave him a life and made him believe there is hope and light in the future which made the young man realise there is no better life out there in the street Most of the masses went on drug pushing, prostitution, robbery, kidnapping, fraud to survive the economy of the present government because that was the only way for quick cash and survival. Priscila and the only son of late mrs katerine the deceased became more close, inlove and addicted to each other. The young man never had a dream of getting back on track to school when there was no hope and future for the masses beacuse of the economy crisis in the country and the whole world at large which transpired after the sudden demise of his mother, so priscila took this challenge upon her self for she was heartbroken of the situation surrounding the only son of mrs katerine. These two individuals never met or seen eachother in physical before but only communicates on facetime and voice calls on the green app. The young man was inspired and supported in many ways to get back on track to school and his dreams
