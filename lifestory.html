A true life of a queen

                  At about noon african time, there was a great rain fall in the city of Abuja Nigeria on an election day where a new government was sworn in. Everyone was scared of the sudden changes and tribulations which left many Nigerian citizens in great grif,fear and regret. Most citizens testified it was a sign of a bad omen because of how strange and sudden it all occured in just less than 24hrs. Prices of food stuff became unaffordable for the masses life became worst and the future became so dark and dozzy. These unbearable conditions led to the lost of lifes and properties, kidnapping and fruad became the only source of survival because there was no hope for the youths and the masses but its said that the youth are the leaders of tomorrow which turned the mind of the masses never to believe in that quote again. At exactly 10pm in the night a beautiful woman was rushed to the pharmacist by her only son for some medical care tho she was ill and diagnosed of maleria fever. After some couple of hours she passed away because the health personal refused treating her for some financial reasons then life became meaningless without the existance of a mother. The only son of the beautiful woman was abandoned by friends and family which made the orphan more stroger, dedicated and never to giveup on his dreams.
                  Few months later, he met a beautiful woman on the green app named Priscila who gave him every reason to smile again and every reason to be succesful. she inspired him, gave him a life and made him believe there is hope and light in the future. Most of the masses went on drug pushing, kidnapping, fraud to survive the economy of the present government because that was the only way for quick cash and survival.
