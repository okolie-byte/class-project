A true Life of a Queen


deeds of a queen

              At about noon african time, there was a great rain fall in the city of Abuja Nigeria on an election day where a new government was sworn in. Everyone was scared of the sudden changes and tribulations which left many Nigerian citizens in great grif,fear and regret. Most citizens testified it was a sign of a bad omen because of how strange and sudden it all occured in just less than 24hrs. Prices of food stuff became unaffordable for the masses, life became worst and the future became so dark and dozzy. These unbearable conditions led to the lost of lifes and properties, kidnapping and fruad became the only source of survival because there was no hope for the youths and the masses but its said that the youth are the leaders of tomorrow which turned the mind of the masses never to believe in their selves again. At exactly 10pm in the night a beautiful woman was rushed to the pharmacist by her only son for some medical care,tho she was ill and diagnosed of maleria fever. After some couple of hours she passed away. The health personal refused treating her because of some financial reasons then life became meaningless without the existance of a mother. The only son of the beautiful woman was abandoned by friends and family which made the orphan more stronger, dedicated and never to giveup on his dreams.
              Few months later, he met a beautiful woman on the green app named Priscila who gave him every reason to smile again and every reason to be succesful. she inspired him, gave him a life nevertheless, he believes in God and hope for a better future which he realises there is no better life being in the street. Most of the masses went on drug pushing, prostitution, robbery, kidnapping, fraud to survive the economy of the present government although, that was the only way for quick cash and survival. Priscila and the only son of late mrs katerine the deceased became more close, inlove and addicted to each other. The young man never had a dream of getting back on track to school when there was no hope and future for the masses beacuse of the economy crisis in the country and the whole world at large. This transpired after the sudden demise of his mother,These two individuals never knew, met or seen eachother in physical before but only communicates on facetime and voice calls on the green app. The young man was inspired and supported by the woman in many ways to get back on track to school and his dreams.

First Day in School

Its been a successful 3 years in school under the department of ICT Computer Science tho there were many challenges at a point he wanted giving up. He was loved and highly respected by his course mates and teachers in the department of ICT. Things became little bit tight and rough for him where he hardly feeds. He struggled with these battles but at same time was scared of giving up on his dreams. During weekends, the young man normally goes to the farm site to make some little money for the weekends, transportations, foodstuffs, school bills and other social amenities. The only way he got a mysterious relaxation and a perfect well being was the voice of mrs Priscila. Mrs Priscilla is a beautiful single mother who got lots of grandkids for she is a queen of a beautiful family. At a point, no female in his own country and the neighborhood was attractive to him anymore. He never believed he was addicted and in love with mrs Priscila who is far older than him.
